Let's Play Crystal Count!


A random counter game that's almost fun!


Directions


  1. - You will be given a random number to start the game.
  2. - There are 4 crystals to choose from.
  3. - Clicking each crystal will add a certain amount of points to your total score.
  4. - You win by adding your total score to equal the random number provided.
  5. - The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click the crystal.
  6. - The value of the 4 crystals will change each time the game resets.
